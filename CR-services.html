Why does my desktop keep overheating?

Desktop computers can overheat due to several reasons. Dust build-up in the fans, insufficient cooling, or inadequate airflow can all cause problems. The CPU, graphics card, and other components can generate a lot of heat, and if they are not cooled properly, they can cause the computer to overheat. Additionally, the power supply can be a source of heat, and if it is not adequately ventilated, it can cause the computer to overheat. To avoid the problem, it is important to regularly clean the fans, check for proper airflow and ventilation, and use a good quality fan and heat sink.
